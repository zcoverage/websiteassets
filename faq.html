Healthcare & FAQs

Healthcare Basics

What is health insurance?

Health insurance helps cover medical expenses, reducing your financial burden when you need care.

Why do I need it?

It protects you from high unexpected medical costs and gives you access to preventive services.

ACA & Marketplace

What is the Affordable Care Act (ACA)?

The ACA expands access to affordable health insurance through subsidies and Marketplace plans.

What is a Marketplace plan?

These are ACA-compliant plans available on HealthCare.gov or your state exchange.

Enrollment & SEP

What is Open Enrollment?

This is the yearly window when you can enroll in or change your health plan.

What is a Special Enrollment Period (SEP)?

An SEP allows you to enroll outside Open Enrollment due to life events (marriage, job change, etc.).

Life & Supplemental

Do you offer life insurance?

No, we focus on health, dental, vision, ACA, and Medicare coverage—not life insurance.

What about dental & vision?

We provide dental and vision options that can be bundled with health coverage or purchased separately.

Broker & Agent Guidance

How can a broker help me?

Brokers simplify plan selection, explain benefits, and ensure you enroll correctly—at no extra cost.

Are you licensed agents?

Yes, we are licensed health insurance agents authorized to assist with ACA, Medicare, and supplemental plans.

Support & Resources

How do I get help?

You can contact us by phone, email, or schedule an appointment for personalized guidance.

Does it cost more to use a broker?

No, brokers are paid by insurance companies, so your cost is the same whether you enroll directly or through us.
